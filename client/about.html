Where to start...

Born and raised in the Greater Toronto Area (GTA), Precious began pursuing a career in the creative fields at the age of 20. The aspiration change came about at the beginning of COVID-19, a time where many individuals were relearning how to live true to themselves. Quickly growing in areas such as illustration, motion design, graphic design, as well as both user interface and user experience designing, she took upon projects driven by passion and deadlines. Indulging in creative outputs is something Precious loves to do most, and in combination with her interests that lie in fashion, gaming, space, and technology, the entire world seems to work as an inspiration to give her work the feeling of in hopes of envoking child-like wonder in all the eyes that are able to view. Currently 23, it is her goal and hope to be able to live up to her name.
